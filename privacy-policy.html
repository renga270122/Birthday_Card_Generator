Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

Soulvest B'day Card Generator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

The app operates entirely on your device and:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not share data with third parties

Local Storage

The app stores the following preferences locally on your device only:

  • Voice language preferences (for speech-to-text features)
  • Voice speed settings (for text-to-speech features)

This data never leaves your device and is only used to enhance your experience.

User-Generated Content

When you create birthday cards:

  • All content (names, ages, messages, photos) remains on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • You control sharing through your device's native share functionality

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Fonts to display text, which may involve requests to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy applies to these requests: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage/Photos: To allow you to upload photos for birthday cards (optional)
  • Microphone: For speech-to-text features (optional)

All permissions are optional and only used for their stated purpose. No data is transmitted externally.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach. All app functionality occurs locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
